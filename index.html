Yardbase is a free and open API of local, community-based data. Contribute and use it to build apps and sites that make your communities better.

Yardbase is Free, Libre Open Source Software under the MIT License. Please help the project by improving its code, design, documentation, and any other rough edge you might find!

Install

Join Madison in installing your city's own Yardbase instance. It's free to run on heroku (up to a point). After that, you'll only have to pay the fine folks at Heroku $15 per month for up to 2GB of data!

Use

Write your own applications that leverage Yardbase's extensive dataset. All you need is a static site to begin mashing up our data!
